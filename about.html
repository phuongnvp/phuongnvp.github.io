Nguyen Van Phuong

I got interested in chemistry pretty early and was lucky enough to win some prizes during high school - a silver medal at the 44th International Chemistry Olympiad (IChO) in USA and several national awards. After high school, I studied at Hanoi University of Pharmacy, graduating with the highest degree, and then went on to complete my master's degree at the same university.

I'm really excited about interdisciplinary research - where different scientific fields come together to solve complex problems. I believe that interesting discoveries will happen when we combine knowledge from various areas. Currently, my main focus is on using computational techniques in chemistry and drug discovery. It's fascinating to see how computer science, chemistry, biology, and other fields can work together to tackle challenging questions in drug development.

When I'm not working on research, I love getting lost in good novels. Some of my favorites are Game of Thrones by George R. R. Martin, The Three-Body Problem by Liu Cixin, and The Sorrow of War by Bao Ninh.
