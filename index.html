Welcome to Natural Products lab at Department of Pharmacognosy, Hanoi University of Pharmacy. Our interdisciplinary research combines computational methods, including molecular modeling and machine learning alongside experimental approaches to unlock the therapeutic potential of natural products.

We welcome all students passionate about natural products research and drug discovery whether you're interested in computational approaches, experimental work, or both. Research experience is not required as long as your enthusiasm to learn and explore the fascinating world of natural products.

Current students at all levels (undergraduate, graduate, and postdoctoral researchers) interested in joining our lab are encouraged to contact Prof. Nguyen Thu Hang or Nguyen Van Phuong
